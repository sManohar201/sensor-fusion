# ROS2 Module 12 Quiz - Field Deployable Stack

## Q1

What is a degraded mode?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

A controlled behavior mode where the robot continues limited operation after a subsystem fault while maintaining safety.

</details>

## Q2

Why should recovery behavior be bounded?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Unbounded retries can hide persistent failures, consume resources, and delay safe operator intervention.

</details>

## Q3

What should final deployment documentation include?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Startup procedure, configuration selection, expected topics, health checks, failure modes, recovery actions, bagging policy, and shutdown procedure.

</details>
