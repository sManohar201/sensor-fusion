# ROS2 Module 11 Quiz - Observability & Operations

## Q1

Why are logs not enough for robot health?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Logs are unstructured and human-oriented. Health systems need structured diagnostics that can be consumed by tools and automation.

</details>

## Q2

What makes a diagnostic actionable?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

It identifies the affected subsystem, severity, evidence, and likely operator or recovery action.

</details>

## Q3

Why record bags during field failures?

<details><summary>Answer</summary>

Bags preserve the data needed to reproduce and diagnose behavior after the robot returns from the field.

</details>
